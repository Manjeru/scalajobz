Premium Job Policy

Post your job on Scalajobz a community driven job portal for Scala jobs. There have been multiple requests in the past to allow for Sticky job posting. Please read https://github.com/knoldus/scalajobz/issues/17

What is Premium Job?

It is a sticky job for pages 1,2 and 3. There are 2 premium jobs per page(for first three pages only) which would show on the top of the page and stay there for next 10 days.

Why do I need a premium job post?

  • Maximise the visibility of your job postings and attract more candidates to your jobs.
  • It would be visible right on the top always for the next 10 days.
  • Job post receives a distinguished level of exposure on the jobs page by appearing with a  .
  • Your job will be amongst the top  results in the relevant search filters.
  • Premium jobs have a place of their own, they do not scroll down like other jobs when new jobs are added.
  • All the people who have signed for job mailers, get the premium job everyday along with their job search criteria in their email.

Premium Job Posting Packages

  • Page 2: $198
  • Page 3: $98
